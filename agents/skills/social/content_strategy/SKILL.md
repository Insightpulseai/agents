# Skill: Content Strategy

**Skill ID**: social.content_strategy
**Agent**: SocialMediaManager (agent_008)
**Version**: 1.0.0

## What It Is

Develops content pillars, themes, and strategic frameworks that guide what the social media manager communicates, to whom, and through which channels. Translates brand objectives into actionable content direction.

## When to Use

- Starting a new brand or campaign content plan
- Defining or refreshing content pillars
- Mapping content to audience segments and funnel stages
- Quarterly or monthly strategy planning sessions

## Core Pattern

```
Input: Brand guidelines + campaign objectives + audience data
Process:
  1. Identify 3-5 content pillars from brand identity
  2. Map pillars to audience segments
  3. Assign funnel stages (awareness → conversion)
  4. Define content mix ratios per platform
  5. Identify seasonal/trending opportunities
Output: Content strategy document with pillars, mix ratios, and calendar skeleton
```

## Content Pillar Framework

| Pillar Type | Purpose | Example |
|------------|---------|---------|
| Educational | Build authority, provide value | How-to guides, tips, frameworks |
| Inspirational | Emotional connection, aspiration | Customer stories, vision posts |
| Promotional | Drive action, convert | Product features, offers, launches |
| Community | Build belonging, engage | Polls, Q&As, UGC reposts |
| Cultural | Show brand personality | Behind-the-scenes, team highlights |

## Mix Ratio Guidelines

| Platform | Educational | Inspirational | Promotional | Community | Cultural |
|----------|------------|---------------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| LinkedIn | 35% | 20% | 15% | 15% | 15% |
| X/Twitter | 25% | 15% | 15% | 30% | 15% |
| Instagram | 20% | 30% | 20% | 15% | 15% |
| Facebook | 25% | 20% | 15% | 25% | 15% |
| TikTok | 25% | 25% | 10% | 20% | 20% |

## Inputs

- Brand voice guidelines document
- Campaign objectives and KPIs
- Audience persona definitions
- Historical performance data (if available)
- Competitive landscape notes

## Outputs

- Content pillars with descriptions
- Platform-specific content mix ratios
- Funnel-stage mapping per pillar
- Calendar skeleton (themes per week/month)
- Recommended posting cadence per platform
