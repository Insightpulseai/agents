# Instagram Direct — Hypotheses

Insights that need more data to confirm.

## H1: 200 DM/hour rate limit will push businesses toward higher-quality, segmented automation

- **Observation:** Meta reduced API limits from 5,000 to 200 DMs/hour (96% reduction)
- **Hypothesis:** This forces businesses to be more selective with automated messages, potentially improving user experience and engagement rates
- **Evidence needed:** Compare engagement/conversion rates before and after rate limit change
- **Confidence:** Medium

## H2: Click-to-DM ads outperform traditional lead ads for high-ticket items

- **Observation:** 74% of adults feel more connected to messageable brands; 21x conversion improvement with fast response
- **Hypothesis:** For products/services over $500, click-to-DM ads generate higher ROI than form-based lead ads
- **Evidence needed:** A/B testing data across price points
- **Confidence:** Medium-High (supported by Sephora/Kiehl's case studies)

## H3: The 24-hour messaging window creates a natural SLA that improves customer satisfaction

- **Observation:** Businesses can only message within 24 hours of user's last message
- **Hypothesis:** This constraint actually benefits CX by forcing rapid response, which aligns with the 75% of consumers expecting responses within 24 hours
- **Evidence needed:** CSAT scores for businesses using IG Direct vs. email support
- **Confidence:** Medium

## H4: Ice breakers significantly reduce conversation drop-off for first-time messagers

- **Observation:** Up to 4 FAQ-style questions can be configured as conversation starters
- **Hypothesis:** Accounts with well-configured ice breakers see 30%+ higher conversation completion rates
- **Evidence needed:** Before/after data from accounts implementing ice breakers
- **Confidence:** Low-Medium

## H5: Conversational commerce via Instagram DMs will outpace traditional IG shopping

- **Observation:** Product carousels in DMs, "Shop Now" buttons, and projected $290B in conversational commerce
- **Hypothesis:** DM-based product discovery and purchase will generate higher AOV than Instagram Shop browsing
- **Evidence needed:** Comparative conversion and AOV data
- **Confidence:** Medium
